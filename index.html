Welcome to our research group at the School of Physics, East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST). Our research focuses on high-energy physics phenomenology, including jets, perturbative QCD, effective field theory, and top-quark physics.

Research Areas

  • Jets & Perturbative QCD: High-precision theoretical predictions for jet production in collider experiments
  • Small-x QCD & Color Glass Condensate: Understanding QCD dynamics at high energies and high parton densities
  • Top-quark Physics: Precision measurements and theoretical calculations involving top quarks
  • Effective Field Theory: Applications of EFT methods to high-energy processes
  • AI/ML for High-Energy Physics: Developing machine learning surrogates for theoretical predictions
