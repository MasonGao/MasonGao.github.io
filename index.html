Welcome! We are based at the School of Physics, East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST). Our research focuses on high-energy physics phenomenology, including jets, perturbative QCD, effective field theory, and top-quark physics, with a particular emphasis on the intersection of artificial intelligence and high-energy physics.

For more information about Gao's background and publications, please see the Curriculum Vitae. You can also explore our Research, Publications, Talks, and People pages to learn more about the group.
