Welcome to Meisen Gao Group at the School of Physics, East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST). Our research focuses on high-energy physics phenomenology, including jets, perturbative QCD, effective field theory, and top-quark physics.

Recent work emphasizes small-x QCD and Color Glass Condensate (CGC) physics, BK evolution fits to HERA data, and the development of artificial intelligence methods as surrogates for theoretical predictions, highlighting the intersection of AI and high-energy physics.

Research Areas

  • Jets & Perturbative QCD: High-precision theoretical predictions for jet production in collider experiments
  • Small-x QCD & Color Glass Condensate: Understanding QCD dynamics at high energies and high parton densities
  • Top-quark Physics: Precision measurements and theoretical calculations involving top quarks
  • Effective Field Theory: Applications of EFT methods to high-energy processes
  • AI/ML for High-Energy Physics: Developing machine learning surrogates for theoretical predictions
